# LLM Response Evaluation Rubric

This rubric is used to evaluate the quality of responses generated by
large language models for the STEM reasoning prompt dataset.

Responses are evaluated across three criteria:

## 1. Accuracy
Measures whether the final answer is correct.

Score Guide:
3 – Correct answer  
2 – Partially correct answer  
1 – Incorrect answer  

---

## 2. Reasoning Quality
Measures whether the explanation logically supports the answer.

Score Guide:
3 – Clear step-by-step reasoning  
2 – Some reasoning but incomplete or unclear  
1 – No reasoning or incorrect reasoning  

---

## 3. Clarity
Measures how understandable and well-structured the explanation is.

Score Guide:
3 – Clear, well-structured explanation  
2 – Mostly understandable but somewhat vague  
1 – Confusing or poorly explained  

---

## Final Score

Total Score = Accuracy + Reasoning Quality + Clarity

Maximum Score: 9

Responses scoring:
7–9 → Strong response  
4–6 → Moderate reasoning quality  
1–3 → Weak or incorrect response
